# Competitor Analysis
**Fitness Wearables — Positioning Reference (2024–2025)**

---

## Positioning Map

FitByte sits between Garmin (technical/complex) and Apple Watch (lifestyle/limited battery):
- More insight-focused than Garmin
- Better battery and sport metrics than Apple Watch
- More capable than Fitbit, without the subscription-only model of Whoop

**Our white space:** The "serious but sane" category — users who want real data without a PhD to read it.

---

## Key Differentiators to Emphasize in Content

1. **Battery life** — 14 days (Pro) vs 18 hours (Apple Watch). Use this contrast.
2. **Dual-frequency GPS** — concrete accuracy story vs generic "GPS tracking"
3. **6-stage sleep tracking** — more granular than Fitbit's basic 3-stage model
4. **Recovery Score** — single actionable number vs Garmin's complex readiness dashboard
5. **Plain language insights** — "go easy today" vs a wall of metrics
6. **No required subscription** — everything accessible without FitByte+ (unlike Whoop)

---

## Messaging to Avoid

- Don't name competitors directly in marketing copy (per brand guidelines)
- Don't claim "most accurate" without specific data to back it up
- Don't position against Apple Watch on design — compete on capability and battery
