# GreenPulse Brand Guidelines

## Company Overview
GreenPulse is an eco-tech company that creates smart home products designed to help homeowners reduce energy waste and live more sustainably.

## Brand Voice
- Friendly and approachable
- Hopeful and optimistic
- Practical and down-to-earth
- Tech-savvy but easy to understand
- Encouraging, never judgmental

## Target Audience
Environmentally-conscious homeowners aged 25-45 who want practical ways to lower energy usage and modernize their homes.

## Tagline
Smart Living, Green Future

## Messaging Principles
- Focus on empowerment, not guilt
- Make sustainability feel achievable
- Highlight convenience and cost savings alongside environmental benefits
- Avoid fear-based or alarmist language
- Use clear, simple explanations over technical jargon

## Preferred Tone
- Warm and optimistic
- Clear and educational
- Confident but not pushy
- Conversational, like a helpful neighbor

## Words We Love
- efficient, sustainable, smart living
- energy-saving, greener future, connected home
- practical, simple, empowering

## Words to Avoid
- guilt, sacrifice, complicated
- revolutionary, disruptive, game-changing
- cutting-edge (overused)
