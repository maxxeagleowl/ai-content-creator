## Health & Fitness Features

### Continuous Tracking (24/7)
- Heart rate — continuous optical monitoring with BPM alerts (high and low)
- SpO2 — on-demand (Core/Pro) or continuous overnight (Ultra)
- Skin temperature — baseline deviation tracking
- Stress score — derived from HRV; updated every 5 minutes
- Move reminder — alerts after 50+ minutes of inactivity
- Menstrual cycle tracking — period prediction, symptom logging, fertility window

### Sleep Tracking
- Six sleep stages: awake, REM, light, deep, drowsy, restless
- Sleep score (0–100) based on duration, stage quality and consistency
- Snore detection (via microphone, opt-in)
- Breathing rate monitoring
- Sleep coaching — weekly tips based on 30-day trends
- Smart wake alarm — wakes user during lightest sleep stage within a 30-minute window

### Recovery
- Daily Recovery Score (0–100) — factors in HRV, sleep quality, resting heart rate and recent training load
- Body Battery — real-time energy reserve indicator updated continuously
- Recommended intensity level for the day based on recovery score
- HRV status — 7-day trending baseline with deviation alerts

### Workout & Activity
**Tracked automatically (auto-detection):** walking, running, cycling, swimming, strength training, elliptical, rowing, yoga, climbing stairs

**Manual sport profiles (Core: 30+, Pro: 60+, Ultra: 80+):** includes running, cycling, triathlon, open water swimming, trail running, skiing, snowboarding, golf, tennis, HIIT, pilates, martial arts and more

**Running metrics:**
- Pace, distance, cadence, stride length
- Ground contact time and balance (Pro/Ultra)
- Vertical oscillation and ratio (Pro/Ultra)
- Running power (Pro/Ultra)
- Race predictor — estimated finish times for 5K, 10K, half marathon, marathon
- Course navigation with turn-by-turn directions

**Cycling metrics:**
- Speed, distance, elevation, cadence
- Power meter support via ANT+ and Bluetooth (Pro/Ultra)
- VO2 max — cycling-specific estimate

**Strength training:**
- Automatic rep counting
- Set detection and rest timer
- Exercise library (200+ movements)
- Muscle map — visual post-workout summary

### Performance Metrics
- VO2 Max — estimated from running and cycling workouts; updated per session
- Training Load — acute and chronic load tracking; flags overreach and undertraining
- Training Readiness Score — combines recovery, sleep, HRV and load into a daily go/no-go metric
- Lactate Threshold — estimated from heart rate during tempo efforts (Pro/Ultra)
- Performance Condition — real-time comparison to baseline during a workout
- Heat and altitude acclimation status (Pro/Ultra)

## Connectivity & Smart Features

### Connectivity
- Bluetooth 5.3
- Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n (2.4 GHz)
- ANT+ (Pro/Ultra)
- NFC payments
- Satellite (Ultra — SOS + two-way messaging)

### Notifications
- Call, text and app notifications (iOS and Android)
- Reply from wrist (Android)
- Do not disturb and focus mode scheduling
- Emergency SOS — automatically contacts emergency services after hard fall detection

### Navigation
- Preloaded topographic maps (Pro/Ultra)
- Turn-by-turn routing synced from the app
- Back-to-start navigation
- Downloadable trail and route maps
- Ski resort maps with run tracking (Pro/Ultra)

### Music
- On-device storage: Core (4GB / ~500 songs), Pro (8GB / ~1,000 songs), Ultra (16GB / ~2,000 songs)
- Spotify, Apple Music and Deezer sync for offline playback
- Bluetooth headphone control from wrist

### Payments
- FitByte Pay — NFC contactless payments via Visa, Mastercard and Amex
- Supported in 45+ countries

## FitByte App

Available on iOS 16+ and Android 12+.

### Dashboard
- Daily summary: activity, heart rate, sleep and recovery in one view
- Trend charts: 7-day, 30-day and 12-month views for all metrics
- Health snapshot — shareable weekly report (PDF)

### Insights Engine
- Personalized daily insights based on the previous 30 days of data
- Anomaly detection — flags unusual readings with plain-language explanations
- Goal recommendations — adapts weekly step, sleep and active minute targets based on progress
- Readiness coaching — daily guidance on effort level, rest and hydration

### Social & Community
- Activity sharing with friends and followers
- Group challenges (step count, active minutes, streak-based)
- Leaderboards (opt-in)
- Strava, Apple Health and Google Fit integration

### Third-Party Integrations
- Strava, Garmin Connect, TrainingPeaks, Wahoo
- Apple Health, Google Fit
- MyFitnessPal (nutrition and calorie correlation)
- Calm, Headspace (stress and mindfulness sync)
